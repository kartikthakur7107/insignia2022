IT-Quiz

WEB DESIGNING

2) Students will bring there own laptops for designing the web page.

IT EXTEMPORE

We are excited to organize IT EXTEMPORE COMPETITION as the part of Technical events.

1)Only one student can participate.

2)Participant has to speak only in English.

3)Maximum time limit to think over the topic will be 2 minutes and the maximum time for speech will be 4 minutes.

4)Participants will not be allowed to take any paper or material with him or her during the speech.

LOGIC BUILDING

Complete description of LOGIC BUILDING COMPETITION and its details are -

1) Two members per team will be allowed.

2) Total duration to complete the program will be of 1 hour.

3) Program to test the logic will be given on spot.

4) Language used to solve the given problem can be C/C++/Java/Python.

5) Judgment of this event will be based on solution strategy used to solve the problem.

correctness, completeness, time/ space requirements and user interface of the program.

NET SAVVY

Complete description of NET SAVVY COMPETITION and its details are -

1) Maximum 2 members per team will be allowed.

2) Participants have to search relevant information regarding the topic given on spot. After that a document in MS Word and a presentation in MS PowerPoint is to be prepared.

3) Relevance of content and its presentation will be the major consideration.

4) Total time allotted will be 1 hour.

TREASURE HUNT

TREASURE HUNT- A game in which players in a group have to hunt the hidden treasure Description:

1)Maximum number of participants allowed is 3-5. The team may consist of all boys all girls or a mixture of both.

2)Each team will have to follow the directions written on their clues.

3)Maximum time allowed is 40 minutes.

4)The team taking the minimum amount of time to discover the treasure and handover to the event coordinator will be declared as winner.

5)The team interfering in other team clues will be disqualified..

BALLOON DART!

TREASURE HUNT- A game in which players in a group have to hunt down the balloons with dars Description:

1) There will be balloons lined up in a track.

2) The student must pop his/her balloon as quickly as possible by sitting on it, stepping on it, or by other means.

3) There will be time limit for popping as many balloons as possible.

4) The student who pops maximum balloons in the required time will be the winner.

5) Only one student can participate.
